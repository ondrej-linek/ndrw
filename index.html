About me

19 years old, from Czechia
I am a graphic designer operating in the static between clear communication and total abstraction. My work doesn’t just sit on the wall; it vibrates. I treat the poster format as a laboratory – testing the limits of legibility, distorting type, and introducing controlled chaos to traditional grids. I believe that in a world of clean, corporate minimalism, the most radical thing you can do is make some noise.
